Sunflower

When it blooms:

Late summer to early fall

Why we love them:

They're fun and come in every color and size from tiny to gigantic! Plus, they're easy to grow from seed, though you may need to protect the seeds and seedlings from birds and rodents with a chicken wire cage formed around them.

Tulip

When they bloom:

Early to late spring

Why we love them:

Their stately blooms announce that spring has arrived! Plant the bulbs in the fall, but be aware that they're often iffy about coming back in subsequent years. Treat them as annuals and replant new bulbs the next fall.
